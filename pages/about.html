About
The Owner

Sarah Horowitz

Sarah Horowitz, the Founder and CEO of Noble Niche Executive Recruitment, is a seasoned HR C-Suite executive with a global reach and a knack for driving organizational effectiveness through sourcing the best talent for you. Her 15 years of leadership experience in various industries have consistently spurred growth and expansion, positioning her as a strong partner for your needs.

Sarah’s entrepreneurial drive founded a global recruitment firm known for personalized service and sustainable outcomes. Her multilingual skills and global experience make her an ideal partner for companies and candidates.

SNoble Niche Executive Recruitment emerged from Sarah’s vision for a boutique firm prioritizing positive experiences for candidates and clients. The boutique setting enables focused searches, deeper relationships, and successful placements.

Sarah observed over the years that many firms lack understanding of clients’ and candidates’ true pain points due to a transactional approach and a lack of practical industry experience. She and her partners aim to change this through transparency, empathy, and authenticity.

Sarah derives immense satisfaction from advancing careers and identifying top-tier talent for clients. The Company’s core principles drive talent sourcing in specific markets and industries. She values authentic partnerships and anticipates future collaborations to shape candidates’ futures and clients’ success.

Core Principals

Transparency

We believe the best experiences and outcomes for both clients and candidates are created through full transparency. Having spent years experiencing and observing broken recruitment processes, our goal is to bring a modern and transparent solution to our stakeholders.

Empathy

Our deliberate effort to understand and resonate with the emotions and experiences of our clients and candidates, creating a business culture that values and responds to the human element in all its interactions.

Execution

Our unwavering commitment to turning needs into successful outcomes through decisive actions and the disciplined management of resources, ensuring that your business objectives are achieved with precision and efficiency.

Passion

Re-discover the power of passion with us. It’s the driving force behind our relentless pursuit of talent matching and the commitment that ensures we deliver exceptional value to you.
